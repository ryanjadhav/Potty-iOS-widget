# Future Enhancements

Here are some features that would complement a potty training app well:

## Tracking & Insights
- Daily/weekly stats — charts showing frequency, patterns, and trends over time
- Streak counter — celebrate consecutive dry days to motivate both parent and child
- Time-of-day patterns — identify when accidents or successes are most likely

## Motivation & Rewards
- Sticker/reward system — kids earn virtual stickers or stars for successful trips
- Celebration animations — confetti or fun sounds when logging a success
- Milestone badges — "First dry day!", "One week streak!", etc.

## Practical Helpers
- Accident logging — track misses alongside successes to see the full picture
- Custom reminder intervals — adjust the 45-min timer per child's needs
- Multiple child support — families with siblings in different stages
- Notes field — jot down context ("asked to go on their own!", "at grandma's house")

## Sharing
- Export/share reports — send a summary to a pediatrician or partner
- Multi-caregiver sync — iCloud sync so both parents and daycare can log events
